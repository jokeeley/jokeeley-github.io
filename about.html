About Me

In May 2021, I graduated from Western Kentucky University with a BA in English: Technical Writing and a minor in Creative Writing. Since then, I have worked as a copywriter and digital production specialist, with a special focus in eCommerce and marketing. On the job, I've gained a working knowledge of SEO and UX.

January of 2023, I enrolled in the Commonwealth Coders course. During the course, I became proficient in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, and more.

Growing up, I always wanted to be a witch. While coding isn't magic per se, I think it's the closest thing to it. I've discovered a real passion for creating programs and pages that look as good as they function. I would love to bring my unique skill set to your next project.
